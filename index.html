Zi Wei Dou Shu

Zi Wei Dou Shu is a classic Eastern star system.

Zi Wei Dou Shu uses stars, palaces and time as its core framework. By observing the combinations formed by stars in different positions, it interprets personality patterns, life domains and changing phases. Zi Wei Star-Picking inherits this star-based language and applies it to divination for specific questions.

★

Major and auxiliary stars

Zi Wei Dou Shu centers on fourteen major stars, supported by many auxiliary stars. Each star carries its own symbolism and can correspond to personality, situation, relationship and action tendency.

宫

Twelve palaces

The twelve palaces correspond to different life fields, such as self, career, wealth, relationships and movement, allowing star symbolism to land in concrete life contexts.

化

Star interactions

Zi Wei Dou Shu values the interaction among stars and the changes created by strength, placement and transformation. Yuxin Master uses these meanings to clarify the key thread of a present question.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How is Zi Wei Star-Picking related to Zi Wei Dou Shu?

They share the same star tradition. Zi Wei Star-Picking uses star symbolism to enter a specific question and provide an immediate reading for that matter.

What questions are suitable?

Career progress, relationship communication, wealth opportunities, cooperation judgment and timing choices are all suitable for Star-Picking reference.

How should I ask for a better reading?

A specific, focused question works best. For example, "Is this cooperation suitable to push forward this month?" is clearer than "What will happen to me?"

Is the result the final answer?

The result is for cultural entertainment and personal reflection. It does not constitute legal, medical, investment, financial or other professional advice.
